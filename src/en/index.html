About

Who we are

Cabañas Mi Cata Hermosa was born from a passion for welcoming travelers and sharing the best of Catamarca. Carolina, the host, is deeply in love with the culture and landscapes of the province, which she has explored for over 20 years as a photographer.

Since 2022, she offers accommodation with attentive and personalized service, in a property that now features two cabins designed so that every guest feels comfortable and supported by the knowledge of a local host.
